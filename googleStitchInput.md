## App: TextFlow SMS
A premium iOS app that schedules SMS/iMessage reminders, templates, quick contacts, history, recurring schedules, analytics, and time suggestions. iCloud sync. Monetization via paywall with RevenueCat.

- SF Pro typography, SF Symbols icons.

## Paywall Rules
- Skip allowed.
- Yearly plan only has 3-day trial.
- Welcome offer lifetime = $20.
- Country pricing handled by App Store localized pricing.
